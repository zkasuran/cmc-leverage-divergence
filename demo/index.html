The Rival-Killer

Beats buy-and-hold on return in bear markets

The obvious challenge: "you only win on drawdown." Split each deep-history asset by regime (price above/below 200-day MA). In down markets the strategy BEATS buy-and-hold on return everywhere, right when an allocator actually needs it. The honest flip side, stated plainly: in a sustained bull it gives up upside and does not beat buy-and-hold on raw return. The universal, every-asset win is lower drawdown, not more return.

The Finding

Confirmation predicts, contrarian is backwards

Crypto folklore says deeply negative funding = "buy the bottom." We tested it. Leverage-confirmed momentum (+14.2% mean 30d, 73% hit) vs the buy-the-dip setup (+2.8%, 53%). The contrarian bucket is worst, monotonic at 30 days (the 7-day ordering is weaker). The 128 confirmed-up windows overlap, so read n as observations, not independent episodes.

The Placebo Test

Is the finding real, or just price momentum?

A confirmed-up bucket beating flush-down could be funding, or it could be that "price rising" alone predicts. We settle it by permutation: keep the price path, permute the funding series with a circular block permutation that keeps funding's own autocorrelation intact (a plain i.i.d. shuffle would overstate significance), and recompute the spread on hundreds of draws. The null is what momentum alone earns with realistically persistent but misaligned funding. Pooled across the liquid majors the real spread clears it at p = —, and the naive i.i.d. shuffle agrees. Per asset it passes where leverage is liquid (ETH, SOL, DOGE) and is honestly absorbed by momentum where it is not. npm run placebo, and npm run verify re-derives the pooled spread.

Real Data, Not A Proxy

Funding is not a price transform

A common shortcut: never fetch funding, just derive it from price momentum (0.0001 + 0.02·return₇). We ran the same strategy with real Binance funding vs that proxy. On the 6 largest assets (liquid funding and CMC20's heaviest weights) real funding wins 5/6, mean Sharpe +0.18. We fetch real funding for all 19 hedgeable constituents. We never synthesise it.
